Recipe categories

Dal Makhani

Dal Makhani is a popular and delicious North Indian dish that is a staple in many households and restaurants. It is a rich and hearty lentil curry made with black gram (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma), simmered in a thick and creamy tomato-based gravy. The dish is characterized by its velvety texture and robust flavor.

Stuffed Paratha

Stuffed paratha is a popular Indian flatbread that is made by stuffing a variety of fillings into unleavened wheat dough and then rolling it out into a round shape before cooking on a griddle or skillet. Parathas can be enjoyed at any mealtime and are often served with yogurt, pickles, or a side of raita.

Dhokla

Dhokla is a popular and nutritious steamed cake originating from the Indian state of Gujarat. It is a fermented dish made primarily from fermented rice and chickpea flour (besan). Dhokla is known for its light and spongy texture and is often served as a snack, breakfast item, or part of a meal.
